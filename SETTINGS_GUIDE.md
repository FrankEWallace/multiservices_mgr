## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### "Changes not showing up"
1. Click "Save Changes" button
2. Wait for success message
3. Refresh the page if needed

### "Currency symbol looks wrong"
- Check **Symbol Position** setting
- Verify **Currency Symbol** is correct
- Ensure **Currency Code** matches your region

### "Company name still shows 'Meilleur'"
- Go to Settings ‚Üí Company Branding
- Change **Company Name** field
- Click **Save Changes**
- Refresh the page

### "Sidebar shows wrong initials"
- The system uses first letters of each word in Company Name
- "Acme Corporation" ‚Üí "AC"
- "The Best Shop" ‚Üí "TB"

## üéì Best Practices

### 1. Complete Your Profile First
Before adding data, set up your company branding. This ensures all exports and reports look professional from day one.

### 2. Use Consistent Formatting
Stick to one currency and date format throughout. Mixing formats causes confusion.

### 3. Review Settings Monthly
Business changes? Update your settings:
- New fiscal year?
- Changed working days?
- Updated contact info?

### 4. Test Export Features
After setting up, generate a test report to ensure:
- Company name appears correctly
- Currency formats properly
- Contact info is complete

### 5. Enable Useful Notifications
Start with all notifications ON, then disable ones you don't need.
